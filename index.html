Domain7
Designer

2014 — Present

I plan and lead clients through the creative process by way of workshops, design exercises, and thoughtful, human-first visual and user experience design.

I also work with a team of project managers, strategists, UX specialists, and developers using agile methodologies to bring our projects through to completion. Other areas of focus have included creating motion prototypes to showcase user interactions to help better articulate ideas; Testing design assumptions through user tests; Spending time as a front end developer writing production ready HTML/CSS/JS when necessary to help lighten the load of the front-end development team.

A key highlight for me was being the creative brand lead for a product of Domain7’s called Civil Space. I executed the visual identity phase as well as the creation of the product’s marketing website. Civil Space was acquired by Zen City in 2021.
